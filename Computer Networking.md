# What is Computer Networking?

Computer Networking is the *communication between computers or devices*.  
It allows devices to *share software, files, information, and even hardware*.

---

## Examples of Networking:

1. *Sharing Software*  
   - Games, movies, applications, etc.

2. *Sharing Files*  
   - Documents like Word files, Excel sheets, PDFs, etc.

3. *Information Sharing*  
   - Similar to chatting on WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook, Twitter  
   - Example: “I bought a new house, its location is …”  
   - Communication must be *secure, preserved, and encrypted*.

4. *Creating Backups*  
   - Example: You accidentally delete a PDF file, but your friend has a copy you shared earlier.

5. *Sharing Hardware*  
   - Example: Connecting your laptop to an LED or projector via HDMI cable.

---

## Key Points for Quick Revision

- Communication between devices  
- Sharing Software  
- Sharing Files  
- Sharing Information  
- Security of Data  
- Information Preservation  
- Sharing Hardware  
- Sharing Data
